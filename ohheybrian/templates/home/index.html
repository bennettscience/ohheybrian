Oh, hey.

I'm Brian Bennett: A collaborative educator and leader focused on using innovative instructional methods to design authentic learning experiences for all students.

I am currently the Coordinator of Professional Learning for Elkhart Community Schools in northern Indiana.

about

I started teaching at an international school in 2009. My principal had enough confidence that a newly-graduated biology education major would be able to teach high school chemistry. Since then, I've taught chemistry, physics, biology, and some introductory computer science. I'm a teacher at heart but also have experience as an instructional coach and district-level team leader for professional development and curriculum design.

When I'm not teaching, I'm working on our family's farm in Michigan. We raise animals, produce, flowers, and honey bees. We're carving out a well-cared for space and teaching our kids the value of hard work.

In my other spare time, I like making things on the Internet.
