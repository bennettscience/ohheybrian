An affordance of technology is to create and use alternative assessment and reporting methods with students. One system that has a large impact on teaching and learning is Standards-based Grading, where student progress is reported in terms of skills demonstrated rather than assignments completed.

I have led workshops with teachers and administrators locally and regionally on how to implement Standards-base Grading in their schools and classrooms. The links below will take you to slides and other documents I have created to help guide participants in reflection on assessment and reporting practices.
